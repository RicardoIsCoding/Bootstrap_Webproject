Brad Pitts
Style and Fashion Evolution

Over the years, the 60-year-old actor has captured the hearts of thousands of fans thanks to his wide range of roles in many movies like fight club or Rendezvous with Joe Black, and he’s done it with great fashion sense.In this comprehensive article, we delve deep into the essence of Brad Pitt’s style, exploring the key elements that have made him a fashion icon for decades.

Brad Pitt Young (Early 90s Look)

Brad Pitt At Young Age

The actor and producer had already established the laid-back aesthetic in the early days of this career. At the Red Heat premiere in 1988, the then 24-year-old-actor wore a casual ensemble of jumper and trousers under a leather jacket.

That same year, the young actor also showed his liking for black and white, which is something he maintains throughout his style evolution. Here, he rocks a classic leather bomber jacket, roomy trousers and a basic white T-shirt.

Denim on denim topped with an oversized sports coat was Brad’s style of choice for a red carpet event in 1991. The Babel actor wore this casual-yet-elevated look to the Thelma & Louise movie premiere in Los Angeles. Brad accessorized with a pair of round tinted sunglasses and suede boots – another closet staple of his.

Mid 90s Look (sexiest man alive)

Brad Pitt The Hippy Style

The Hollywood star continued experimenting with his looks. During the True Romance era, Brad continued his affinity for button-up shirts, rolled sleeves and V-necklines. He also welcomed long fairytale prince-like hair, which paired well with that handsome face of his.

That same year, the Fight Club actor continued his grungey style phase, which is synonymous with the ‘90s. This is the longest we’ve ever seen his hair, but of course, he pulls it off because he‘s Brad Pitt after all. For this red carpet event, the actor took his jeans and suede boots to a higher level of relaxed with a loose grey sweater.

Although he radically changed his hairstyle, the movie star kept some of his wardrobe staples when he appeared at VH1 Honors wearing blue jeans and his trusty pair of suede boots. He paired them with a striped vest and a basic long sleeve shirt.

2000s Look (with Jennifer Aniston)

Brad Pitt Streetstyle, baggy pants Phase

Pitt’s pants started getting baggier in the early 2000s.

Pitt was infamous for dressing down for the red carpet in his earlier days. Pitt retired his trusty blue jeans and leather jacket in exchange for fitted suits in 2009.

The Babel actor wore this casual-yet-elevated look to the Thelma & Louise movie premiere in Los Angeles. Brad accessorized with a pair of round tinted sunglasses and suede boots – another closet staple of his.

2010s Look (with Angelina Jolie)

Brad Pitt loves suits

Pitt’s style changed drastically in the late 2000s. He ditched his baggy jeans and bottle blonde hair for a suit and tie. He also started accessorising more with scarves and hats.
